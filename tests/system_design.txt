Q: what is system design? 
A: System design is the process of defining the architecture, components, and interfaces of a system, including th
e relationships between them. It involves identifying the functional and non-functional requirements of the system, and designing
the system to meet those requirements.

Q: What is the purpose of the system design?
A: The purpose of system design is to create a system that meets the requirements of the users and
stakeholders, and is scalable, maintainable, and efficient.
